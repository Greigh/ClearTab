Your account is protected

Boring security, on purpose.

Protected sign-in

A quick Cloudflare Turnstile check keeps automated bots out of the login form. Real users barely notice.

Encrypted passwords

Your password is hashed with bcrypt on the server and is never stored in plain text. Even we can't read it.

Secure sessions

You stay signed in through a private HttpOnly cookie only your browser can use. Changing your password signs out every other device.
