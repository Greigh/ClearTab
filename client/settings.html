Profile

Display name

Shown in the navbar's "Signed in as" panel. Leave blank to fall back to your email.

Time zone

Controls what "today" means when FiHaven counts down to a due date. Defaults to your browser's detected zone — change this if you travel often or want a fixed reference.

Payments

Choose how much you need to pay before a bill or card counts as fully paid. Anything less shows as a partial payment with the remainder still due. Bills always use their full amount; this mainly affects credit cards.
